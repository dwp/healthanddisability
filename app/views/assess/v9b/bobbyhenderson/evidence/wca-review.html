Moving around and using steps

Tomas says they can move safely and repeatedly on level ground without needing to stop for: 100 metres

“I had an accident in July - I was run over and it damaged my left leg. On a good day I can walk 100m slowly with crutches on the flat - I'd have to rest a couple of times though, as I'd be in pain. On a bad day, I can just about get around my flat but would usually stay in bed so I don't have to move much.”

Standing and sitting

Tomas says they can move from one seat to another right next to it without help from someone else.

Tomas says they stay in one place pain free without help for less than 30 minutes.

"I can stand with crutches, but not without them. On a good day I might be able to stand for about 10 minutes but on a bad day it’s hard for me to use crutches at all, so I couldn’t really stand because I can’t put weight on my leg."

"I can sit down but I have to elevate my leg and move it every so often. It gets uncomfortable because of the pain so I end up having to reposition myself."
